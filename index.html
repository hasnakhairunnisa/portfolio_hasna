About Me

Data analyst with strong business context in e-commerce, logistics, and operations.

I’m Hasna, a Senior Data Analyst who enjoys sitting between data, business, and product. I’ve worked on projects that analyze annual company performance, explain why GMV or retention is dropping, segment customers using Python, and design dashboards that executives and teams actually use.

My focus is on turning messy, scattered datasets into narratives that answer concrete questions such as: “Why is our GMV dropping?”, “Which customers should we focus on?”, or “Where exactly are users dropping in our funnel?”
